Experiment Paradigms

  • Layered (CMY/RGB): 3 separate QR codes composited into one image. Each channel decoded independently.
  • FMT: Mathematical transform directly maps 3-bit values to RGB colors.
  • Palette: Single QR with multi-color modules. Nearest-neighbor classification to recover bits.
  • Corner vs Finder: Corner patches provide known calibration colors. Finder-only uses B/W reference from QR structure.
  • RGB vs OKLCH: OKLCH is perceptually uniform - better color discrimination under varying lighting.
