How It Works

  • Sender: Generates 3 QR codes (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow channels) and composites them in a 440x440 square. Adds 4 corner calibration patches (White, Cyan, Magenta, Yellow) at 20px each.
  • Receiver: First detects QR using grayscale conversion (finder patterns are B/W). Uses detected QR position to locate corner patches. Samples corner colors and interpolates all 8 CMY combinations using a linear model.
  • Key insight: By detecting the QR first, we know exactly where calibration patches should be. Linear interpolation from 4 base colors estimates all 8 possible CMY combinations for nearest-neighbor classification.
